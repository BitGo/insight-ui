RMG chain

RMG Chain is the digital platform for holding and trading Royal Mint Gold.

RMG Chain is singularly focused on the security and digitization of a single asset: gold held in 100% reserve at The Royal Mint. The blockchain is designed for safety from the ground up:

  • All wallets are multi-signature. Security experts have long recognized the value of multi-signature wallets. Single-key losses are not possible on the RMG blockchain.
  • No “black holes”. Due to the way the blockchain enforces keys, it is impossible to send funds to an invalid address where the RMG could not be recovered.
  • All parties participating on the network are identified before being allowed to transact.
  • The blockchain exclusively utilizes trusted, proven technology. It does not contain smart contracts, experimental cryptographic functions, or algorithms that have not already been heavily tested and proven in existing blockchain systems.

RMG Chain is in alpha testing and is on track to launch in the second half of 2017. This site will provide additional updates on the availability of the block explorer in the coming months.
